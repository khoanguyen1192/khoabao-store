## 2. Privacy-First Architecture: Data Collection Framework

### 2.1 Offline-First Design Philosophy

AuraDesk is engineered as a **privacy-by-design** productivity and widget studio tool that operates primarily offline on your iOS/macOS device. The App is designed to function independently without requiring internet connectivity for core functionality.

### 2.2 No Personal Data Collection

**We do NOT collect, harvest, store, or transmit any of the following:**

- Personal Identifiable Information (PII) such as name, email address, phone number, or postal address
- Health or fitness metrics, biometric data, or medical information
- Location data or device GPS coordinates
- Contact lists, photos, or media library contents
- Calendar events or personal notes (beyond what you explicitly input as countdown event titles)
- Browser history or internet usage patterns
- Device identifiers, advertising identifiers (IDFA), or device fingerprints

### 2.3 Locally Stored Data

Data you create within the App—such as countdown event titles, target dates, emoji selections, and theme preferences—is stored locally on your device using Apple's native SwiftData framework. **This data remains entirely under your control and is never transmitted to Khoa Bảo Store servers or third-party analytics platforms.**

### 2.4 Widget Configuration Data

Widget configurations, theme selections, and customization preferences are stored in your device's App Groups container (`group.com.khoabao.auradesk`). This shared container enables seamless synchronization between the AuraDesk main app and WidgetKit extensions but remains confined to your device.

## 3. Third-Party Services & Apple StoreKit 2 Integration

### 3.1 Apple App Store & StoreKit 2

AuraDesk uses Apple's **StoreKit 2** framework to facilitate in-app purchases for premium themes and subscription products. When you purchase premium content:

- **All transaction processing, billing, and payment handling** is managed directly by Apple Inc. through the App Store
- **Your payment card and billing information** is processed exclusively by Apple's secure payment infrastructure—Khoa Bảo Store never receives, stores, or processes your financial details
- **Transaction records and purchase history** are stored by Apple and synchronized to your iCloud account according to Apple's standard privacy and security policies

For comprehensive details regarding Apple's handling of transaction data, please refer to:
- [Apple App Store Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/)
- [Apple StoreKit Documentation](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/storekit/)

### 3.2 App Store Review Requests

AuraDesk optionally displays App Store review prompts using Apple's native `SKStoreReviewController`. These prompts are managed entirely by Apple's system and do not transmit any personal data to Khoa Bảo Store. Apple's system collects aggregate review submission data; for details, consult Apple's privacy documentation.

### 3.3 No Third-Party Analytics

AuraDesk does **not** integrate any third-party analytics services, crash reporting platforms (Fabric, Sentry, Firebase Analytics), advertising networks, or user tracking services. We do not employ SDKs that harvest device data, behavioral analytics, or engagement metrics.

## 4. Data Retention & Security

### 4.1 Local Data Retention

Data you create within AuraDesk is retained locally on your device for as long as you maintain the App installed. You may delete this data at any time by:

1. Manually deleting countdown events within the App's interface
2. Clearing the App's data through iOS Settings → General → iPhone Storage → AuraDesk → Offload App or Delete App
3. Performing a factory reset of your iOS device

### 4.2 No External Data Retention

Since AuraDesk does not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on external servers, there is **no external data retained** by Khoa Bảo Store beyond Apple's native StoreKit transaction records (which Apple manages).

### 4.3 Security Measures

- **Encryption in Transit:** All communication with Apple's StoreKit infrastructure uses industry-standard TLS 1.3 encryption
- **Encryption at Rest:** Locally stored data on your device is protected by iOS/macOS hardware-level encryption (Secure Enclave)
- **No Third-Party Sharing:** Khoa Bảo Store does not share, sell, rent, or license your data to advertising brokers, data brokers, or external parties
- **Code Security:** The App is built with secure coding practices and reviewed against OWASP Top 10 vulnerability guidelines

## 5. Your Privacy Rights

### 5.1 California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

- **Know** what personal data is being collected (though AuraDesk collects no PII)
- **Delete** your local data at any time via the App or device settings
- **Opt-out** of data sales (AuraDesk does not engage in data sales)
- **Non-discrimination** for exercising your privacy rights

### 5.2 European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

If you are an EU resident, you have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data (local data accessible within the App; Apple transaction data via Apple)
- **Rectification** of inaccurate data
- **Erasure** (right to be forgotten) by deleting the App or clearing its data
- **Data portability** of your countdown events via manual export
- **Withdraw consent** by uninstalling the App

For GDPR-related inquiries regarding Apple's transaction processing, contact Apple directly via their privacy portal.

### 5.3 Other Jurisdictions

AuraDesk complies with applicable privacy laws in all jurisdictions where it is distributed. If your jurisdiction grants additional privacy rights (e.g., LGPD in Brazil, PIPEDA in Canada), those rights apply equally.
